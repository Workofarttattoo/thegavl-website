How It Works

Chrono-Walker applies temporal reasoning to your case:

  1. Ingests current case state and historical case progression data
  2. Models how likely events (discovery, motions, settlement) will impact case
  3. Projects probability distributions across multiple future timelines
  4. Identifies high-probability branch points and decision windows
  5. Delivers actionable timeline for maximizing case strength
